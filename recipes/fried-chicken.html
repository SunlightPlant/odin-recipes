Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Description

Very tender and delcious fried chciken thanks to it's buttermilk.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of white pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried sage
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups of buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degree F
  2. Place buns in a single layer in the air fryer basket
  3. Cook in the preheated air fryer until crips (about 2 minutes)
  4. Remove buns to a plate
  5. Place hot dogs in a single layer in the air fryer basket
  6. Cook in the air fryer for 3 minutes
  7. Serve hot dogs in toasted buns
