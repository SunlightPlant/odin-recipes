Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Description

Very tender and delcious fried chciken thanks to it's buttermilk.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried sage
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups of buttermilk
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 and 1/2 quarts peanut oil

Steps

  1. Toss chicken pieces, black pepper,salt, paprika, white pepper, rosemary, thyme, oregano, sage,and cayeene together in a large bowl
  2. Stir in buttermilk until chicken is evenly coated.Cover and refrigerate for 6 hours.
  3. Combine flour, salt, paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, white pepper, and onion powder in a large shallow dish
  4. Remove chicken from buttermilk and dredge each piece in seasoned flour; shake off any excess and transfer to a plate
  5. Heat peanut oil in a large oven to 350 degrees F
  6. Add chicken pieces to the hot oil and fry for 10 minutes. Turn chicken pieces and fry for another 10 to 15 minutes.
  7. Transfer fried chicken to a cooling rack set over a paper towel-lined baking sheet. Let it sti for 10 minues before serving.
