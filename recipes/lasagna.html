World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is considered by many food critics to be the greatest lasagna in the world. It includes delicious vegatables and meats and is very customizable, meaning that you can alter the lasagna's ingredients to your needs.

This recipe was created by John Chandeler over 20 years ago.

Ingredients

This recipe is very customizable so feel free to change some ingreidents to your needs

  • Meat - Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Tomatos - A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Spices and seasoning - Fresh parsley,dried basil leaves,salt,Italian seasoning,fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses - Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
